How to Convert a Handwritten Essay to Text

1. Download Pen to Print

2. Scan and Convert

Use the Pen to Print app to scan a handwritten page and convert it into text.

3. Save as a Photo

Once converted, save the page as a photo.

4. Extract Text on iOS

On iOS, open the photo, press and hold the text, then select and copy it.

5. Paste into Notes

Paste the copied text into a note. Repeat this for each page of the essay.

6. Clean Up the Text

Delete unnecessary characters from diagrams and remove unwanted line breaks.

7. Copy and Paste into the Essay Analyzer

Once the text is cleaned up, copy and paste it into the essay analyzer.
