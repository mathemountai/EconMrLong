How to Convert a Handwritten Essay to Text

1. Download a Handwriting to text converter

Use a handwriting app. For example, Pen to Print is a quality app that will scan multiple handwritten pages and convert it into text.

2. Import Pdf Files

If you have converted text in a Pdf,then you can simply upload or drag and drop the Pdf file.

3. Extract Text on iOS

Alternativley save the page as a photo. On iOS, open the photo, press and hold the text, then select and copy it.

4. Directly, paste into the Leveller

You can paste and delete unnecessary characters from diagrams and remove unwanted line breaks.
